## 1. What we collect

**a. Account information** (registered accounts): email address; password (stored only as a
salted cryptographic hash — we cannot read it); sign-in tokens. Guest accounts: a device-linked
identifier only, until you register.

**b. Profile information**: display name; avatar selection; country (optional); in-game handicap,
XP, career tier and progression; equipment and purchases made with in-game currency.

**c. Gameplay data**: rounds, sessions and scores; every shot's inputs and server-validated
outcomes (club, power, positions, distances, lie, spin); per-shot analytics used for your
statistics (for example distance-to-pin and surface before/after each shot); saved mid-round
games; shot replay records.

**d. Purchase data** (when in-app purchases launch): the items you bought, store transaction
identifiers and entitlements. **Payment is processed by Apple or Google — we never receive your
card details.**

**e. Technical data**: IP address; device type and operating system version; app version;
language/locale; request identifiers, timestamps and server logs; crash and error diagnostics;
connectivity state (for offline-play sync).

**f. Support data**: correspondence when you contact us.

**g. Push notification tokens** (only if/when you enable notifications).

**Website (FourXGames.com):** the Site uses only strictly necessary cookies [CONFIRM — if
analytics or embedded content are added, this section and a cookie banner will be required].

We do **not** collect: real names (unless you put one in your display name), precise location,
contacts, photos, biometric data, or advertising identifiers.

We do **not sell** personal information and do **not share it for cross-context behavioural

- **Infrastructure providers (processors):** Amazon Web Services, Inc. — cloud hosting of our

- **App stores:** Apple and Google, when they process your purchases, subscriptions or refunds
  under their own privacy policies, and for app distribution and (if enabled) push notifications.
- **Professional advisers and authorities** where required by law, to enforce our terms, or to
  protect the rights, safety and integrity of players and the Services.
- **A buyer or successor** in a merger, acquisition or asset sale — with notice to you and

- **Other players** see only what the game shows: your display name, avatar, country flag (if

All processors act under contracts (data processing agreements) that restrict them to processing
on our instructions with appropriate security.

## 8. Security

We protect personal information with, among other measures: encryption in transit (TLS);
passwords stored as salted bcrypt hashes; short-lived, signed access tokens; server-side
authoritative game logic (clients cannot write scores directly); per-service database isolation;
the principle of least privilege for staff access; and logging and monitoring. No system is 100%
secure; if a breach creates a risk to you, we will notify you and the relevant regulator
(including the Information Regulator under POPIA section 22 and GDPR Article 33/34 timelines)
without undue delay.
