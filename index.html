About

FourX Games

FourX Games Proprietary Limited is an independent studio registered in the Republic of South Africa (registration number 2026/552177/07). Our first title, Prestige Golf, is a skill-first mobile golf game launching in late 2026.

Prestige Golf is built on a fair-play promise: shot outcomes are computed and validated server-side, competitive modes use only real players, and nothing you buy changes the physics of a competitive shot. The game carries no advertising and no third-party tracking SDKs.

Common questions

How do I recover guest progress?

Guest progress is tied to your device. Upgrade to a registered account (email or Apple/Google sign-in) as early as possible so your career is saved and syncs across devices.

How do refunds work?

In-app purchases are handled by Apple or Google. Refund requests are processed by the relevant store under its policies. Your non-excludable statutory rights are unaffected — see the Terms, clause 6.

How do I report a bug or a cheater?

Email support@fourxgames.com. Please don't exploit bugs — report them and we'll fix them.

Account

Delete your account & data

You can permanently delete your Prestige Golf account and associated personal data at any time, in one of two ways.

Option 1 — In the game

Open Prestige Golf and go to Settings ▸ Account ▸ Delete account, then confirm.

Option 2 — By request here

If you can't access the game, email privacy@fourxgames.com from the address on your account with the subject line “Delete my account”. We may need to verify your identity before we proceed.

What is deleted, and when

  • Deleted or de-identified within 30 days: your profile, display name, avatar, progression, gameplay and shot records, virtual items and purchase entitlements.
  • Deleted from backups within the normal backup rotation shortly afterwards.
  • Retained where the law requires it: purchase and transaction records are kept for tax and company-law purposes (South Africa: generally 5 years), de-linked from your identity where feasible.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Full detail is in our Privacy Policy (section 5 & 6).

Request deletion by email

Send to privacy@fourxgames.com with subject “Delete my account”.
